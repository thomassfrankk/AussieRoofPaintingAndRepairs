Roof Cleaning Sydney

Restore your roof's appearance with a professional deep clean.

Years of exposure to Sydney's elements leaves your roof covered in dirt, moss, lichen, algae and built-up grime. Beyond looking unsightly, a dirty roof can actually cause damage — moss and lichen trap moisture against the surface, accelerating wear and potentially leading to leaks and tile deterioration.

Professional roof cleaning removes all of that buildup and restores your roof's original appearance. It's also an essential first step before any roof painting or coating job, ensuring proper adhesion and a long-lasting finish. Our team uses commercial-grade high-pressure equipment to safely and effectively clean tile, metal and Colorbond roofs across Sydney.

What we offer

  • High-pressure roof cleaning
  • Moss, lichen and algae removal
  • Valley and edge clean-up
  • Debris removal from roof and surrounding areas
  • Post-clean inspection

A clean roof looks better, lasts longer and protects your home. Fully insured, no hidden costs.
