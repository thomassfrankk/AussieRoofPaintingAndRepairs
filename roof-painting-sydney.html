Roof Painting Sydney

Transform and protect your roof with a premium paint job built to last.

Your roof is one of the first things people notice about your home. Over time, Sydney's harsh sun, heavy rain and coastal winds can fade and wear down even the toughest roof surfaces. A professional roof paint job does more than just improve the look of your property — it adds a protective layer that extends the life of your roof by years.

At Aussie Roof Painting & Repairs, we specialise in tile and metal roof painting across Sydney. Our process starts with a thorough high-pressure clean to remove all dirt, moss and debris, followed by any necessary repairs and re-pointing. We then apply a premium primer and two coats of industry-leading roof paint from trusted brands, available in a wide range of colours to suit your home.

Our Process

  • High-pressure roof clean and surface preparation
  • Repair and re-pointing of damaged areas
  • Primer application
  • Two coats of premium roof paint
  • Final inspection and clean-up
